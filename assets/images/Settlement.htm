      How to reduce the differential Settlement under a road embankment

      • Report this article
      Luqman Khan

      Luqman Khan

      Technical Construction Manager at A.S. Khan Construction Pvt ltd Islamabad.

      Published Jun 28, 2015
      + Follow

      For reducing the differential settlement under a road embankment some steps must be taken as described below,

      1. Reinforcing layer

                       When designing the foundation structures that will have to sustain the  concentrated loading, as for a large road, two main problems should be focused. a)    First of all the possibility of unsustainable loading has to be considered, a lot amount of consolidation settlement should be expected under both the first and second enlargement.

      b)     The execution of vertical drains and application of preloading for nine months under the new enlargement will surely contribute to improve the situation. 

      The possibility of the tension cracking of asphalt pavement should also be expected. In fact due to the high stiffness of the pavement as compared with the high compressibilty of the lower soil, localized cracking could generate. Also for this reason, high stiffness well compacted stabilized should be coupled with Geogrids.
