application

Applicable for embankment and cutting slopes of superficial deposits. For repair settlement it is re-elevation of the road using Asphalt as a patching without exceeding the bearing capacity load of the foundation. In the case of contaminated land and/or insufficient space, the regrading will be steep angle with a sheet pile to achieve the regrading slope. To high slope it is not applicable due to the high cost .In the case of flattened slope, it requires sufficient space and awareness of the existence other assets (e.g. drainage system, utilities) without encroachment on private properties .


Remarks



It is used to increase the soil strength through increase its cohesive parameter. It addresses the geology factor through increasing the soil resistance by amending the soil with admixtures such as lime, fly ash and cement, as a result it reduce the impact of the environment factor on the slope.
